The GPSR is now mandatory.

Since December 2024, any seller based outside the EU (USA, Canada, Turkey and beyond) must appoint an EU Responsible Person to keep selling to European consumers.

01

Customs seizures

Shipments without a valid Responsible Person can be held, returned or destroyed at the EU border.

02

Heavy fines

National authorities can impose significant penalties for trading without a compliant RP in place.

03

Marketplace removal

Etsy, Amazon and Shopify already delist listings that lack a registered Responsible Person.
